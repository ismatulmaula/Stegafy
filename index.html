stegafy is a free online steganography app

What is steganography?
Steganography is, according to the most trusted of sources, the art and science of writing hidden messages in such a way that no one, apart from the sender and reciepient suspects the existance of the message.

In the days of the Ancient Greeks this meant shaving the head of a slave, tattooing a message onto his head, and waiting for the hair to grow back! In the wake of modern technology steganography has evolved into hiding messages in data, one good example being pictures.

stegafy is an app that lets you encode and decode messages within pictures. To encode, simply select the encode radio button, input a message, an optional password, and select a picture (must be at least 35x35). To decode, select a picture (encoded by stegafy) and the password with which the message was encoded with.
